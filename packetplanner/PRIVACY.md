# **Privacy Policy - PacketPlanner (Angpao Planner)**


**Effective Date:** 2026-02-09


## **1. Introduction**


PacketPlanner / Angpao Planner (\"the App\") is provided by Modern Logic Tech Solutions (\"we\" or \"us\"). We prioritize your privacy and transparency.


## **2. Data Collection & Storage**


**Local Storage Only:**


The App stores all your data **locally on your device**. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.


* **Recipient Lists:** Your recipient names, packet amounts, and groups are stored locally using device storage (Capacitor Preferences API and browser localStorage).
* **Settings:** Your language preference, theme choice, and currency symbol are stored locally.
* **Calculator Data:** Note types and inventory counts are stored locally.


**No Cloud Sync:**


Unlike some apps, PacketPlanner does not synchronize data across devices or upload data to any cloud service.


## **3. Payments & In-App Purchases**


**One-Time Purchase:**


The App offers a one-time \"Pro\" upgrade that unlocks:
- Unlimited groups (Free users limited to 1 group)
- Export to Excel
- Backup/Restore data
- Ad-free experience (Android only)


* **Payment Processing:** All financial transactions are processed directly by the **Apple App Store** or **Google Play Store**. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card information or billing details.
* **Transaction Data:** We may receive anonymous transaction receipts to validate your \"Pro\" status. This data does not include your personal identity.


## **4. Third-Party Services**


**Google Play Services & Apple StoreKit:**
* Used for purchase validation on their respective platforms.
* We do not collect analytics or tracking data through these services.


**AdMob (Android Free Users Only):**
* The free version of the Android app displays banner ads at the bottom of the screen provided by Google AdMob.
* AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad targeting. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.
* Pro users do not see any ads.


## **5. Permissions**


The App requires the following permissions to function:


* **Storage Access (Android):** Required to export/import backup files and Excel reports.
* **Network Access:** Required to validate premium licenses and serve ads (free Android version only).


## **6. Data Deletion**


Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:
* **iOS:** Settings → General → iPhone Storage → PacketPlanner → Delete App
* **Android:** Settings → Apps → PacketPlanner → Storage → Clear Data


## **7. Children's Privacy**


The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect data from users, the app is safe for all ages.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the \"Effective Date\" at the top of this policy.
