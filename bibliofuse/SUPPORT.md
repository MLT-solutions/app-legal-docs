# Support - BiblioFuse Reader

**Need Help?**

We are here to assist you with any issues or questions regarding BiblioFuse Reader.

## Contact Support
Please email us at: **support@mlogictech.com**

## Reporting Issues
When reporting a bug or crash, please copy/include the following information to help us solve it faster:
1.  **Device Model** (e.g., Pixel 7, Samsung S23)
2.  **Android Version** (e.g., Android 13)
3.  **App Version** (Found in Settings > About)
4.  A brief description of what you were doing when the issue occurred.

Thank you for using BiblioFuse!
